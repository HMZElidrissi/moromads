## Structural SEO Priorities

1. **City pages are the core asset** — `/casablanca`, `/fez`, `/agadir` should be server-rendered with proper `<title>`, `<meta description>`, and `LocalBusiness` JSON-LD schema for each spot (WiFi, hours, address, rating).

2. **Use "nomad" framing** — "digital nomad Morocco" gets 3–4x more searches than "remote work Morocco". Use it in headings and meta descriptions.

3. **Schema markup** — `LocalBusiness` JSON-LD on each spot page makes you eligible for Google rich results.

---

## Biggest Gap: Missing Cities

**Marrakech and Rabat** are the two highest-search-volume Moroccan cities for this use case and are not in the dataset yet. Marrakech alone is the single highest-ROI addition.
